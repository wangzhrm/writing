雅思写作Task 1：柱状图专题

掌握描述柱状图的必备句式和技巧。

1. 描述整体特征 (Describe the Overall Features)

这部分用于在文章开头概述图表的总体特征或最显著的特点。

  • Overall, it is clear that the largest number of students were enrolled in Arts.
  • A striking feature of the chart is that the most popular subject for male students is Science.
  • In general, the data shows that the number of online users saw a dramatic increase over the period.
  • The most noticeable feature of the chart is that the sales figures for product A were consistently higher than all others.
  • Generally, mobile phone sales accounted for the largest proportion of total sales in all years.

2. 描述详细数据 (Describe the Detailed Data)

这些句式可以帮助你精确地描述图表中的具体数据点。

2.1 描述具体数值

  • The number of cars produced in Japan stood at 12 million.
  • 150 tonnes of waste were recycled in the first quarter.
  • The figure for oil consumption was approximately 30 million barrels.
  • Exactly 500,000 tourists visited the country in August.
  • Sales of digital cameras reached a peak of 2.5 million units.

2.2 描述最大值、排名及最小值

  • The highest number of participants was recorded in 2015, at 800,000.
  • The second most popular category was leisure activities, with 30% of respondents.
  • The lowest figures were seen in January, with only 200 sales.
  • Rent made up the majority of the monthly expenses.
  • Football was the most preferred sport, followed by basketball.

3. 描述趋势 (Describe the Trend)

这些句式可以帮助你描述数据随时间的变化。

  • The number of subscribers showed a gradual increase from 100,000 in 2010 to 300,000 in 2020.
  • There was a sharp decline in the percentage of landline usage, falling from 60% to 15% over the decade.
  • The number of car rentals fluctuated, but it ended the period at a higher figure.
  • The figure for mobile phone sales saw a significant rise from 20 million units in 2005 to 50 million in 2015.
  • The percentage of people using public transport decreased steadily from 45% to just 25% over the five-year period.

4. 进行比较 (Make Comparison)

这些句式可以帮助你对比图表中的不同数据点。

  • The number of tourists in Country A was twice as high as the number in Country B.
  • A similar pattern can be seen in the sales of product X and product Y, with both showing similar growth.
  • The gap between the sales figures for Product A and Product B was considerable.
  • The UK was a far more popular destination than Spain.
  • The figures for online advertising and newspaper advertising were roughly similar.
