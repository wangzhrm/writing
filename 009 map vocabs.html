IELTS Writing Task 1: Map Vocabulary

Location and Position

Located/Situated (位于) The park is located in the northern part of the town.
公园位于城镇的北部。
Positioned (定位) The new shopping mall is positioned to the east of the residential area.
新购物中心定位在住宅区的东部。
Adjacent to (毗邻) The library is adjacent to the town hall.
图书馆毗邻市政厅。
Opposite (对面) The school is opposite the supermarket.
学校在超市对面。
Near/Close to (靠近) The factory is close to the river.
工厂靠近河流。
In the vicinity of (在……附近) A new park was built in the vicinity of the housing estate.
在住宅区附近新建了一个公园。
Bordering (接壤) The farmland bordering the forest was converted into a park.
与森林接壤的农田被改建为公园。
Surrounded by (被……包围) The lake is surrounded by residential buildings.
湖泊被住宅楼包围。
Central/Centrally located (中心的/位于中心) The town square is centrally located.
城镇广场位于中心。
On the outskirts (在郊区) The new factory was built on the outskirts of the city.
新工厂建在城市郊区。

Directions

North/South/East/West (北/南/东/西) The railway station is in the south of the town.
火车站位于城镇的南部。
Northeast/Southwest, etc. (东北/西南等) A new road was constructed in the northeast corner of the map.
在地图的东北角新建了一条道路。
To the north/south/east/west of (在……的北/南/东/西) The park was developed to the west of the old factory.
公园在旧工厂的西部开发。
Towards (朝向) The new road extends towards the northern boundary.
新道路朝北部边界延伸。
Along (沿着) A footpath runs along the riverbank.
一条步行道沿着河岸延伸。
Parallel to (平行于) The new railway runs parallel to the main road.
新铁路与主干道平行。
Across (横跨) A bridge was built across the river.
一座桥梁横跨河流建成。
Beyond (超出) The residential area extends beyond the forest.
住宅区延伸到森林之外。

Changes and Development

Constructed/Built (建造) A new hospital was constructed in the western part of the town.
在城镇西部新建了一所医院。
Demolished/Removed (拆除/移除) The old factory was demolished to make way for a park.
旧工厂被拆除以腾出空间建公园。
Replaced by/With (被……取代) The farmland was replaced by a shopping complex.
农田被购物中心取代。
Converted into (改建为) The warehouse was converted into a community center.
仓库被改建为社区中心。
Expanded/Extended (扩展/延长) The residential area was expanded to the north.
住宅区向北部扩展。
Relocated (迁移) The school was relocated to the eastern part of the town.
学校被迁移到城镇的东部。
Developed (开发) The southern area was developed into a commercial zone.
南部地区被开发为商业区。
Renovated/Refurbished (翻新/整修) The old library was renovated to include modern facilities.
旧图书馆被翻新以包含现代化设施。
Transformed (转变) The industrial zone was transformed into a green space.
工业区被转变为绿地。
Added (添加) A playground was added near the school.
在学校附近添加了一个游乐场。

Features and Infrastructure

Building (建筑) Several new buildings were erected in the town center.
在镇中心新建了几栋建筑。
Facility (设施) A sports facility was built near the park.
在公园附近建造了一个体育设施。
Residential area (住宅区) The residential area expanded significantly by 2020.
到2020年，住宅区显著扩展。
Commercial area (商业区) The commercial area now includes a large shopping mall.
商业区现在包括一个大型购物中心。
Industrial area (工业区) The industrial area was relocated to the outskirts.
工业区被迁移到郊区。
Road/Highway (道路/高速公路) A new highway was constructed to connect the town to the city.
新建了一条高速公路以连接城镇和城市。
Path/Footpath (小路/步行道) A footpath was added along the river.
沿河添加了一条步行道。
Bridge (桥梁) A bridge was built to improve access to the northern district.
建造了一座桥梁以改善北部地区的通行。
Railway/Train station (铁路/火车站) The railway station was modernized in 2025.
火车站在2025年进行了现代化改造。
Park/Green space (公园/绿地) A large park was created in the center of the town.
在镇中心创建了一个大型公园。

Size and Extent

Significant/Substantial (显著的/大量的) The town underwent significant development over the decade.
该城镇在十年间经历了显著的发展。
Considerable (相当大的) A considerable area of farmland was converted into housing.
相当大的农田面积被改建为住宅。
Minor/Small-scale (小的/小规模的) A small-scale park was added near the school.
在学校附近添加了一个小型公园。
Extensive (广泛的) The extensive road network improved transportation.
广泛的道路网络改善了交通。
Limited (有限的) The changes to the western part were limited to a new footpath.
西部地区的变化仅限于新增一条步行道。
