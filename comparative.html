形容词的比较级和最高级规则

形容词的比较级用于比较两个事物，最高级用于比较三个或以上的事物。其变化规则主要取决于形容词的音节数量。

规则一：单音节形容词

大多数单音节形容词，直接在词尾加 -er 构成比较级，加 -est 构成最高级。

1. tall - taller - tallest

2. short - shorter - shortest

3. old - older - oldest

4. young - younger - youngest

5. big - bigger - biggest

6. small - smaller - smallest

7. strong - stronger - strongest

8. weak - weaker - weakest

9. fast - faster - fastest

10. slow - slower - slowest

11. rich - richer - richest

12. poor - poorer - poorest

13. nice - nicer - nicest

14. long - longer - longest

15. high - higher - highest

16. low - lower - lowest

17. clean - cleaner - cleanest

18. dirty - dirtier - dirtiest

19. hard - harder - hardest

20. soft - softer - softest

规则二：以字母 -e 结尾的单音节形容词

以字母 -e 结尾的单音节形容词，直接加 -r 构成比较级，加 -st 构成最高级。

1. nice - nicer - nicest

2. large - larger - largest

3. wide - wider - widest

4. late - later - latest

5. fine - finer - finest

6. close - closer - closest

7. safe - safer - safest

8. brave - braver - bravest

9. wise - wiser - wisest

10. simple - simpler - simplest

11. cute - cuter - cutest

12. pale - paler - palest

13. prime - primer - primest

14. rare - rarer - rarest

15. pure - purer - purest

16. free - freer - freest

17. true - truer - truest

18. blue - bluer - bluest

19. rude - ruder - rudest

20. sure - surer - surest

规则三：以 "辅音字母 + y" 结尾的双音节形容词

以 辅音字母 + y 结尾的双音节形容词，先将 -y 变为 -i，再加 -er 构成比较级，加 -est 构成最高级。

1. happy - happier - happiest

2. easy - easier - easiest

3. busy - busier - busiest

4. heavy - heavier - heaviest

5. angry - angrier - angriest

6. lucky - luckier - luckiest

7. healthy - healthier - healthiest

8. pretty - prettier - prettiest

9. ugly - uglier - ugliest

10. funny - funnier - funniest

11. early - earlier - earliest

12. noisy - noisier - noisiest

13. dry - drier - driest

14. tidy - tidier - tidiest

15. hungry - hungrier - hungriest

16. lazy - lazier - laziest

17. dirty - dirtier - dirtiest

18. friendly - friendlier - friendliest

19. lonely - lonelier - loneliest

20. windy - windier - windiest

规则四：以 "单元音字母 + 单辅音字母" 结尾的单音节形容词

以 单元音字母 + 单辅音字母 结尾的单音节形容词，双写最后一个辅音字母，再加 -er 构成比较级，加 -est 构成最高级。

1. big - bigger - biggest

2. hot - hotter - hottest

3. thin - thinner - thinnest

4. fat - fatter - fattest

5. sad - sadder - saddest

6. wet - wetter - wettest

7. red - redder - reddest

8. mad - madder - maddest

9. glad - gladder - gladdest

10. fit - fitter - fittest

11. dim - dimmer - dimmest

12. slim - slimmer - slimmest

13. tan - tanner - tannest

14. glum - glummer - glummest

15. sun - sunnier - sunniest (注意：这个词实际上是双音节词，但符合此规则的拼写变化)

16. trim - trimmer - trimmest

17. sob - sobber - sobbest

18. jog - jogger - joggest

19. wag - wagger - waggest

20. hug - hugger - huggest

规则五：多音节形容词（三个或以上）和部分双音节形容词

多音节形容词和大部分双音节形容词，在其前面加 more 构成比较级，加 most 构成最高级。

1. beautiful - more beautiful - most beautiful

2. difficult - more difficult - most difficult

3. expensive - more expensive - most expensive

4. important - more important - most important

5. comfortable - more comfortable - most comfortable

6. popular - more popular - most popular

7. dangerous - more dangerous - most dangerous

8. famous - more famous - most famous

9. careful - more careful - most careful

10. polite - more polite - most polite

11. delicious - more delicious - most delicious

12. handsome - more handsome - most handsome

13. useful - more useful - most useful

14. serious - more serious - most serious

15. exciting - more exciting - most exciting

16. interesting - more interesting - most interesting

17. intelligent - more intelligent - most intelligent

18. valuable - more valuable - most valuable

19. convenient - more convenient - most convenient

20. peaceful - more peaceful - most peaceful

规则六：不规则变化

有一些常用的形容词不遵循上述规则，需要特殊记忆。

1. good - better - best

2. bad - worse - worst

3. little - less - least

4. much/many - more - most

5. far - farther/further - farthest/furthest

6. old - older/elder - oldest/eldest

7. ill - worse - worst

8. well - better - best

9. late - later/latter - latest/last

10. near - nearer - nearest/next

11. inner - inner - inmost/innermost

12. outer - outer - outmost/outermost

13. good-looking - better-looking - best-looking

14. bad-tempered - worse-tempered - worst-tempered

15. far-reaching - farther-reaching - farthest-reaching

16. little-known - less-known - least-known

17. far-off - farther-off - farthest-off

18. in - inner - innermost

19. out - outer - outermost

20. up - upper - uppermost
