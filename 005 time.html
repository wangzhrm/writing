IELTS Writing Task 1: Time Expressions

Time Points

In (在……期间)

Example: In 2020, the population increased significantly.

例句：在2020年，人口显著增加。

At (在……时刻)

Example: At the beginning of the decade, sales were low.

例句：在十年初，销售额很低。

On (在……那天)

Example: On January 1st, the new policy was implemented.

例句：在1月1日，新政策开始实施。

Time Periods

Over the period (在整个时期内)

Example: Over the period from 2015 to 2020, unemployment rates dropped.

例句：在2015年至2020年的整个时期内，失业率下降。

Throughout (贯穿整个)

Example: Throughout the decade, consumer spending remained stable.

例句：整个十年中，消费者支出保持稳定。

Between ... and ... (在……和……之间)

Example: Between 2000 and 2010, urban population grew rapidly.

例句：在2000年至2010年间，城市人口迅速增长。

By (截至)

Example: By 2025, renewable energy usage had doubled.

例句：截至2025年，可再生能源使用量翻倍。

During (在……期间)

Example: During the first quarter, profits rose steadily.

例句：在第一季度，利润稳步上升。

From (从……开始)

Example: From 2010 onwards, technology advanced rapidly.

例句：从2010年起，技术迅速发展。
