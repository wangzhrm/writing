雅思写作Task 1：描述数据专题

掌握描述饼图的必备句式和技巧。

1. 描述整体特征 (Describe the Overall Features)

这部分用于在文章开头概述图表的总体特征或最显著的特点。

  • Overall, it is clear that there are significant differences between the two cities in terms of daily travel habits.
  • A striking feature of the chart is that the most popular form of travel in both cities is driving.
  • In general, the data shows that the majority of commuters rely on private transport.
  • The most noticeable feature of the chart is that more than half of the respondents prefer online shopping.
  • Generally, sales of electronic devices accounted for the largest proportion.

2. 描述详细数据 (Describe the Detailed Data)

这些句式可以帮助你精确地描述图表中的具体数据点。

2.1 描述具体占比

  • The proportion of students who chose History stood at 25%.
  • 55% of the population travelled by public transport.
  • A third of the total budget was allocated to education.
  • The figure for online shopping was approximately 30%.
  • Exactly a quarter of the total sales came from the European market.

2.2 描述最大占比、排名及最小占比

  • The largest proportion of sales was accounted for by the technology sector, at 45%.
  • The second largest category was leisure activities, which constituted 20% of the total.
  • The smallest share was from healthcare, which only made up 5% of the total expenditure.
  • Farming made up the majority of the land use, at 60%.
  • Rent constituted the largest proportion of monthly expenses, followed by food and entertainment.

3. 描述趋势 (Describe the Trend)

当饼图呈现不同年份或时期的数据时，可以描述趋势。

  • The proportion of online shoppers showed a gradual increase from 10% in 2000 to 30% in 2010.
  • There was a sharp decline in the percentage of people using private cars, falling from 60% to 40% over the decade.
  • The proportion of rent expenditure fluctuated, but it ended the period at a higher percentage.
  • The proportion of online advertising saw a significant rise from 30% in 2010 to 65% in 2020.
  • The percentage of people relying on public transport decreased steadily from 40% in 2005 to just 20% in 2015.

4. 进行比较 (Make Comparison)

这些句式可以帮助你对比图表中的不同数据点。

4.1 饼图内部比较

  • The expenditure on food was twice as high as the amount spent on transport.
  • A similar pattern can be seen in the expenditure on entertainment and utilities, with both accounting for a small proportion of the total.
  • The gap between the proportion of online shopping and in-store shopping was considerable.
  • Reading was a far more popular choice than gaming.
  • The figures for cycling and walking were roughly similar.

4.2 两个饼图之间比较

  • In contrast to the year 2000, the proportion of public transport usage increased significantly in 2020.
  • The percentage of car ownership in City A was marginally lower than that in City B.
  • While the proportion of people who chose cycling was the highest in 2000, it was significantly lower in 2020.
  • The share of online marketing in 2023 was almost double that of 2020.
  • The figure for printed media advertising dropped sharply, from 30% to just 5%.
