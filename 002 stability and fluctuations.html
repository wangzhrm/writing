IELTS Writing Task 1 Vocabulary

Vocabulary for Stability or No Change

Verbs

  • Remain stable
  • Stay constant
  • Level off (leveled off, leveling off)
  • Stabilize (stabilized, stabilizing)
  • Plateau (plateaued, plateauing)
  • Maintain (maintained, maintaining)
  • Hover (hovered, hovering)

Nouns

  • No change
  • A plateau

Example Sentences

  • The price of oil remained stable throughout 2018.
  • After a sharp rise, the figures leveled off in 2019.
  • The percentage of students hovered around 20% for five years.

Vocabulary for Fluctuations

Verbs

  • Fluctuate (fluctuated, fluctuating)
  • Vary (varied, varying)
  • Oscillate (oscillated, oscillating)
  • Experience fluctuations

Nouns

  • Fluctuation
  • Variation
  • Oscillation

Example Sentences

  • The data fluctuated widely between 2010 and 2015.
  • There were erratic variations in temperature over the period.
  • The market experienced significant fluctuations during the crisis.

Exemplary Sentences with Chinese Translations

For Stability or No Change

  • The unemployment rate maintained stability from 2020 to 2022.
    失业率在2020年至2022年间保持稳定。
  • The number of visitors to the museum showed a plateau in 2021.
    博物馆的访客数量在2021年呈现平稳状态。

For Fluctuations

  • Stock prices oscillated significantly during the economic uncertainty.
    在经济不确定期间，股票价格大幅波动。
  • The company’s profits experienced considerable fluctuations over the decade.
    公司在过去十年中的利润经历了相当大的波动。
