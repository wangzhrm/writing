Vocabulary for Describing Trends

Increases

Verbs

  • Rise (rose, risen)
  • Increase (increased, increasing)
  • Climb (climbed, climbing)
  • Grow (grew, grown)
  • Soar (soared, soaring)
  • Surge (surged, surging)
  • Jump (jumped, jumping)
  • Rocket (rocketed, rocketing)
  • Go up (went up, gone up)

Nouns

  • An increase
  • A rise
  • A growth
  • An upward trend
  • A surge
  • A climb

Adjectives/Adverbs

  • Sharp(ly)
  • Dramatic(ally)
  • Significant(ly)
  • Substantial(ly)
  • Rapid(ly)
  • Considerable(ably)
  • Steady (steadily)
  • Gradual(ly)
  • Marked(ly)
  • Notable (notably)

Examples:

  • The number of tourists rose sharply from 2010 to 2015.
  • There was a significant increase in sales over the period.
  • The population grew steadily between 2000 and 2020.

Decreases

Verbs

  • Fall (fell, fallen)
  • Decrease (decreased, decreasing)
  • Decline (declined, declining)
  • Drop (dropped, dropping)
  • Plummet (plummeted, plummeting)
  • Plunge (plunged, plunging)
  • Dip (dipped, dipping)
  • Shrink (shrank, shrunk)
  • Go down (went down, gone down)
  • Dwindle (dwindled, dwindling)

Nouns

  • A decrease
  • A decline
  • A fall
  • A drop
  • A downturn
  • A reduction
  • A slump
  • A dip

Adjectives/Adverbs

  • Sharp(ly)
  • Dramatic(ally)
  • Significant(ly)
  • Substantial(ly)
  • Rapid(ly)
  • Gradual(ly)
  • Steady (steadily)
  • Slight(ly)
  • Marginal(ly)

Examples:

  • The stock prices plummeted dramatically in 2023.
  • There was a steady decline in unemployment rates.
  • Production levels dipped slightly last quarter.

Descriptive Examples

Rapid Rise:

  • Sales rocketed dramatically within a single quarter.
    销售额在一个季度内急剧上升。
  • Website traffic soared sharply in just a month.
    网站流量在一个月内急剧飙升。

Slow Rise:

  • The number of subscribers grew steadily over several years.
    订阅者数量在几年内稳步增长。
  • Revenue rose gradually over the decade.
    收入在十年间逐渐上升。

Rapid Fall:

  • The market value plunged sharply overnight.
    市场价值在一夜之间急剧下跌。
  • Customer complaints plummeted rapidly after the new policy.
    新政策实施后，客户投诉迅速减少。

Slow Fall:

  • Attendance figures dwindled steadily over the season.
    整个赛季的观众人数稳步减少。
  • Production output shrank marginally over several months.
    生产产量在几个月内略微减少。
