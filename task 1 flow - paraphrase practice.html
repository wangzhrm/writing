IELTS Task 1 Paraphrasing Practice: Flow Charts

Use the techniques from the guide (synonyms, structural change, grammar change) to paraphrase the following introductory sentences in **one single sentence**.

Practice 1: Manufacturing

The diagram below shows the steps involved in manufacturing glass bottles.
Exemplar Answer: The schematic illustrates the entire process of how glass containers are manufactured from raw materials.

(Changes: diagram → schematic, shows → illustrates, steps involved in manufacturing → entire process of how, glass bottles → glass containers)

Practice 2: Natural Cycle

The chart shows the life cycle of the salmon.
Exemplar Answer: The provided figure depicts the various stages that the salmon goes through from birth to maturity.

(Changes: chart → provided figure, shows → depicts, life cycle → various stages... from birth to maturity)

Practice 3: Service Process

The diagram below illustrates the process for registering a new student at a university.
Exemplar Answer: The flow chart outlines the steps that must be completed by an applicant to enroll as a new student at a tertiary institution.

(Changes: diagram → flow chart, illustrates → outlines, process for registering → steps that must be completed by an applicant to enroll, university → tertiary institution)
