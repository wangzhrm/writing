Rapid Rise:

  • Sales rocketed dramatically within a single quarter. 销售额在一个季度内急剧上升。
  • Website traffic soared sharply in just a month. 网站流量在一个月内急剧飙升。

Slow Rise:

  • The number of subscribers grew steadily over several years. 订阅者数量在几年内稳步增长。
  • Revenue rose gradually over the decade. 收入在十年间逐渐上升。

Rapid Fall:

  • The market value plunged sharply overnight. 市场价值在一夜之间急剧下跌。
  • Customer complaints plummeted rapidly after the new policy. 新政策实施后，客户投诉迅速减少。

Slow Fall:

  • Attendance figures dwindled steadily over the season. 整个赛季的观众人数稳步减少。
  • Production output shrank marginally over several months. 生产产量在几个月内略微减少。
  • The unemployment rate remained stable from 2020 to 2022. 失业率在2020年至2022年间保持稳定。
  • The number of visitors to the museum reached a plateau in 2021. 博物馆的访客数量在2021年呈现平稳状态。

For Fluctuations:

  • Stock prices fluctuated significantly during times of economic uncertainty. 在经济不确定期间，股票价格大幅波动。
  • The company’s profits experienced considerable fluctuations over the decade. 公司在过去十年中的利润经历了相当大的波动。
