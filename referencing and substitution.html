雅思写作：指代与替换技巧

如何通过指代和替换让你的文章更连贯、更高级

掌握这些技巧可以帮助你在雅思写作中获得更高的分数，使你的文章更加流畅和专业。

指代 (Referencing)

指代即通过代词或替代性词语来指代前文已提及的名词或概念，避免不必要的重复。

常用技巧： 使用 `it`, `they`, `them`, `this`, `that`, `these`, `those` 等代词。其中 `this`, `that` 还可以指代整个句子或观点。

正面例子

The government has a responsibility to protect public health. It should therefore implement stricter regulations on pollution. This would lead to a significant improvement in air quality.

分析： 第二句用 It 指代了 the government；第三句用 This 指代了"实施更严格的污染法规"这一整个想法。

反面例子

The government has a responsibility to protect public health. The government should therefore implement stricter regulations on pollution. The implementation of stricter regulations would lead to a significant improvement in air quality.

分析： 反复重复 the government 和 the implementation of stricter regulations，显得语言非常生硬和冗余。

替换 (Substitution)

替换指的是使用同义词、近义词组或更概括性的词语来代替重复出现的词语，展示丰富的词汇量。

常用技巧：
  • 同义词： `important` → `crucial`, `vital`
  • 同义词组： `pollution` → `environmental degradation`
  • 概括性名词： `cars, buses, trains` → `vehicles`

正面例子

Many youngsters are addicted to social media. This is a pressing issue that needs to be addressed. As a result, parents are increasingly worried about their children's excessive screen time. This phenomenon, if not properly managed, could have long-term negative effects on these individuals.

分析： 用 youngsters, children 和 individuals 来替换，展示了词汇的多样性。用 issue 指代 addiction，使句子更简洁。

反面例子

Many students are addicted to social media. This is a pressing problem that needs to be addressed. As a result, parents are increasingly worried about their children's excessive screen time. This addiction, if not properly managed, could have long-term negative effects on these students.

分析： 重复使用了 students 和 addiction，缺乏词汇变化，让文章显得单调。

练习与纠正

以下句子因没有使用指代或替换而显得不佳。请尝试纠正它们。

  1. Education is very important. Education provides people with knowledge.
  2. Online shopping is becoming popular. Online shopping is more convenient than traditional shopping.
  3. The government has introduced new taxes. The new taxes are designed to curb consumption.
  4. Mobile phones are a problem. Mobile phones distract students in class.
  5. Reading books is a good habit. Reading books can improve vocabulary.
  6. The construction of new roads will help reduce traffic congestion. The new roads will benefit commuters.
  7. The company's profits increased. The company's profits increased by 10%.
  8. Renewable energy sources are the future. Renewable energy sources can combat climate change.
  9. Many people are concerned about air pollution. Air pollution is a major health risk.
  10. The internet has made information widely accessible. The internet has changed how we live.

参考答案与建议

注意这些只是参考答案，您的改写可能有更多种方法！

  1. 原句：Education is very important. Education provides people with knowledge.

    建议：Education is very important as it provides people with knowledge. (使用 it 指代)

  2. 原句：Online shopping is becoming popular. Online shopping is more convenient than traditional shopping.

    建议：Online shopping is becoming popular because this method is more convenient than traditional shopping. (使用 this method 替换)

  3. 原句：The government has introduced new taxes. The new taxes are designed to curb consumption.

    建议：The government has introduced new taxes, which are designed to curb consumption. (使用 which 指代)

  4. 原句：Mobile phones are a problem. Mobile phones distract students in class.

    建议：Mobile phones are a problem because these devices distract students in class. (使用 these devices 替换)

  5. 原句：Reading books is a good habit. Reading books can improve vocabulary.

    建议：Reading books is a good habit as this activity can improve vocabulary. (使用 this activity 替换)

  6. 原句：The construction of new roads will help reduce traffic congestion. The new roads will benefit commuters.

    建议：The construction of new roads will help reduce traffic congestion, and this infrastructure will also benefit commuters. (使用 this infrastructure 替换)

  7. 原句：The company's profits increased. The company's profits increased by 10%.

    建议：The company's profits increased by 10% from the previous year. (避免重复，并提供更详细信息)

  8. 原句：Renewable energy sources are the future. Renewable energy sources can combat climate change.

    建议：Renewable energy sources are the future, as they have the potential to combat climate change. (使用 they 指代)

  9. 原句：Many people are concerned about air pollution. Air pollution is a major health risk.

    建议：Many people are concerned about air pollution because this phenomenon is a major health risk. (使用 this phenomenon 替换)

  10. 原句：The internet has made information widely accessible. The internet has changed how we live.

    建议：The internet has made information widely accessible, and this technological development has profoundly changed how we live. (使用 this technological development 替换)
