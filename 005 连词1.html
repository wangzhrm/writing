IELTS Writing Task 2
Linking Words & Logical Development

1. Sequencing / Ordering Ideas

Function: organise arguments logically

  • firstly / secondly / finally
  • first of all
  • in the first place
  • to begin with
  • subsequently
  • additionally
  • in addition
  • furthurmore
  • moreover
  • after that
  • lastly
  • finally
  • eventually
  • ultimately
  • last but never the least
Example:
Firstly, governments should invest in education. Ultimately, this will benefit society as a whole.
Practice (按逻辑顺序造句):
  1. 政府应改善公共交通，然后再限制私家车的使用。
  2. 学生需要掌握基础知识，之后才能发展更高级的技能。
  3. 个人应先保证身体健康，最后再追求事业成功。
  4. 企业必须先提高产品质量，然后扩大市场。
  5. 人们首先应提高环保意识，最终减少污染。

2. Addition (Adding More Ideas)

Function: add another supporting idea

  • moreover
  • in addition
  • also
  • furthermore
  • not only … but also
  • what is more
Example:
In addition, online education allows students to learn at their own pace.
Practice (在原有观点上补充新观点):
  1. 公共交通可以缓解交通压力，而且能减少空气污染。
  2. 体育锻炼有助于身体健康，还能缓解心理压力。
  3. 阅读可以提高语言能力，同时拓宽视野。
  4. 政府应投资教育，也应改善医疗体系。
  5. 科技不仅改变了工作方式，也影响了人际交流。

3. Cause → Effect / Result

Function: explain reasons and results

  • because
  • due to
  • as a result of
  • therefore
  • thus
  • as a result
  • consequently
Example:
Many people rely heavily on cars; therefore, traffic congestion has become a serious problem.
Practice (说明原因和结果):
  1. 人们过度使用私家车，城市交通变得拥堵。
  2. 学生缺乏睡眠，学习效率下降。
  3. 工厂排放废气，空气质量恶化。
  4. 房价持续上涨，年轻人难以买房。
  5. 政府加大教育投入，学生整体水平提高。

4. Giving Examples / Illustration

Function: support ideas with specific examples

  • for example
  • for instance
  • such as
  • this can be seen when
Example:
For example, many cities have introduced bike-sharing systems to reduce pollution.
Practice (用例子支持观点):
  1. 科技改善了医疗服务。
  2. 公共交通有助于环境保护。
  3. 网络对教育产生了积极影响。
  4. 体育运动对青少年发展很重要。
  5. 政府政策可以改变人们的生活方式。

5. Contrast / Concession

Function: show contrast or balance different views

  • however
  • on the other hand
  • in contrast
  • although
  • even though
  • while
Example:
Although online shopping is convenient, it can harm local businesses.
Practice (表达转折或让步):
  1. 网络学习很方便，但缺乏面对面的交流。
  2. 私家车节省时间，但会造成环境污染。
  3. 城市生活机会更多，但生活成本更高。
  4. 科技发展迅速，但个人隐私受到威胁。
  5. 考试可以评估学生能力，但会带来压力。
