Section 2: Nouns, Example Sentences, and Chinese Translations

General Process-Related Nouns

Process (过程)
The process of manufacturing paper involves several stages.
制造纸张的过程涉及几个阶段。
Stage (阶段)
The first stage of the cycle is the collection of raw materials.
循环的第一阶段是收集原材料。
Step (步骤)
The next step involves heating the mixture to a high temperature.
下一步是将混合物加热到高温。
Phase (阶段)
The cooling phase ensures the product solidifies properly.
冷却阶段确保产品适当固化。
Cycle (循环)
The water cycle describes how water moves through the environment.
水循环描述了水如何在环境中移动。
Procedure (程序)
The procedure for recycling plastic is clearly outlined in the diagram.
回收塑料的程序在图表中清晰概述。
Operation (操作)
The operation of the machine is automated for efficiency.
机器的操作是自动化的，以提高效率。
Activity (活动)
Sorting waste is a key activity in the recycling process.
分类废物是回收过程中的关键活动。
Sequence (序列)
The sequence of steps ensures consistent product quality.
步骤序列确保产品品质的一致性。
Flow (流动)
The flow of water through the system is carefully controlled.
水在系统中的流动受到仔细控制。

Components and Materials

Material (材料)
The material used in production is sourced locally.
生产中使用的材料是本地采购的。
Ingredient (成分)
Each ingredient is carefully measured before mixing.
每种成分在混合前都被仔细测量。
Resource (资源)
The main resource for this process is renewable energy.
该过程的主要资源是可再生能源。
Input (输入)
The input for the machine includes raw cotton and water.
机器的输入包括原棉和水。
Output (输出)
The output of the factory is high-quality fabric.
工厂的输出是高质量的织物。
Product (产品)
The final product is packaged and sent to retailers.
最终产品被包装并送往零售商。
By-product (副产品)
The by-product of the process is used as fertilizer.
该过程的副产品被用作肥料。
Waste (废物)
Waste from production is recycled to reduce pollution.
生产中的废物被回收以减少污染。

Equipment and Tools

Machine (机器)
The machine grinds the raw material into fine powder.
机器将原材料研磨成细粉。
Equipment (设备)
Specialized equipment is required for the filtration process.
过滤过程需要专用设备。
Tool (工具)
A cutting tool is used to shape the metal.
切割工具用于塑造金属。
Device (设备)
The device regulates the temperature during production.
该设备在生产过程中调节温度。
Container (容器)
The liquid is stored in a container before packaging.
液体在包装前储存在容器中。
Conveyor (传送带)
The conveyor transports goods to the next stage.
传送带将货物运送到下一阶段。
Filter (过滤器)
A filter removes impurities from the water.
过滤器去除水中的杂质。
Pump (泵)
The pump moves liquid through the pipes.
泵将液体通过管道移动。
Pipe (管道)
Water flows through a pipe to the treatment facility.
水通过管道流向处理设施。
Valve (阀门)
The valve controls the flow of gas in the system.
阀门控制系统中气体的流动。

Locations and Facilities

Facility (设施)
The facility is equipped with modern machinery.
该设施配备了现代机械。
Factory (工厂)
The factory produces thousands of units daily.
工厂每天生产数千个单位。
Plant (工厂)
The plant specializes in recycling plastic waste.
该工厂专门回收塑料废物。
Station (站点)
Each station in the assembly line has a specific task.
装配线中的每个站点都有特定任务。
Site (地点)
The site for processing is located near the river.
加工地点位于河边。
Area (区域)
The storage area holds raw materials before processing.
存储区域在加工前存放原材料。
Production Line (生产线)
The production line operates continuously.
生产线持续运行。

Natural and Environmental Processes

Reservoir (水库)
Water is stored in a reservoir before treatment.
水在处理前储存在水库中。
Surface (表面)
Rainwater collects on the surface before evaporating.
雨水在蒸发前聚集在表面上。
Atmosphere (大气)
Water vapor rises into the atmosphere during the cycle.
水蒸气在循环过程中上升到大气中。
Precipitation (降水)
Precipitation returns water to the earth’s surface.
降水将水返回到地球表面。
