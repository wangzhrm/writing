2. Proportions and Fractions for Pie Charts

A significant proportion

A significant proportion of the budget was allocated to education in 2020.

2020年，预算的很大一部分被分配给教育。

A substantial share

Healthcare held a substantial share of expenditure in 2021.

2021年，医疗保健占支出的很大份额。

A considerable portion

A considerable portion of energy came from fossil fuels.

能源的相当一部分来自化石燃料。

A small fraction

Renewable energy was only a small fraction in 2000.

2000年，可再生能源仅占一小部分。

A negligible/minimal part

Nuclear energy formed a negligible part in 2015.

2015年，核能在能源组合中占微不足道的部分。

The majority / The vast majority

The vast majority of emissions were from industry.

绝大多数排放来自工业。

A minority / A small minority

A small minority of the budget was for cultural projects.

预算的一小部分用于文化项目。

Half / One-half

One-half of the sales came from the domestic market.

销售额的一半来自国内市场。

A quarter / One-quarter

A quarter of the population used public transport in 2022.

2022年，四分之一的人口使用公共交通。

One-third / Two-thirds

Two-thirds of the revenue was from exports.

收入的三分之二来自出口。

A fifth / One-fifth

One-fifth of energy consumption was from solar power.

五分之一的能源消耗来自太阳能。
