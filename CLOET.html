CLOET Formula for IELTS Writing Task 1

Practice writing your overview paragraphs by addressing each of these key analytical points.

The Formula: CLOET

C

Change

L

Lowest & Highest

O

Obvious Similarities & Differences

E

Exceptional Categories

T

Trend Consistency

C - Change

What is the biggest overall change from the start to the end date?

L - Lowest & Highest

What are the highest and lowest points?

O - Obvious Similarities & Differences

Are there any obvious similarities or differences between the categories?

E - Exceptional Categories

Do any categories stand out as significantly different from the others?

T - Trend Consistency

Is the change consistent, or does it accelerate or slow down?
