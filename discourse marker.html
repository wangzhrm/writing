雅思写作语篇标记词指南

掌握这些标记词，让你的写作更连贯、更有逻辑性

语篇标记词(Discourse Markers)是雅思写作中非常重要的元素，它们可以帮助读者理解不同观点、句子和段落之间的关系。

语篇标记词分类

标示文章阶段

  • 引言/开头
    Firstly, we need to consider the economic impact.
    To begin with, let's examine the historical context.
  • 段落展开/转折
    Next, we should analyze the social consequences.
    However, there are alternative perspectives to consider.
  • 总结/结论
    In conclusion, the evidence strongly supports this view.
    Overall, the trend shows a significant increase.

因果关系

  • As a result
    He studied hard; as a result, he passed the exam.
  • Therefore
    The data is inconsistent; therefore, we need further research.
  • Consequently
    The company lost market share; consequently, profits declined.
  • Thus
    The experiment failed; thus, the hypothesis was rejected.

对比/反转

  • However
    The plan seemed perfect; however, it failed in practice.
  • On the other hand
    Some support this policy; on the other hand, many oppose it.
  • In contrast
    Summer is hot and dry; in contrast, winter is cold and wet.
  • Whereas
    The north is industrial, whereas the south is agricultural.

相似/类比

  • Similarly
    The company expanded into Asia; similarly, it entered European markets.
  • Likewise
    Students must submit assignments on time; likewise, punctuality is expected in exams.
  • In the same way
    Technology has transformed communication; in the same way, it has changed education.

递进/添加信息

  • Moreover
    The plan is cost-effective; moreover, it is environmentally friendly.
  • Furthermore
    The research is thorough; furthermore, it offers practical applications.
  • In addition
    The hotel has spacious rooms; in addition, it offers complimentary breakfast.
  • Not only... but also
    The policy not only reduces pollution but also creates jobs.

举例

  • For example
    Many countries, for example, Sweden and Canada, have high living standards.
  • For instance
    Some sports, for instance, football and basketball, are popular worldwide.
  • Such as
    Renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power, are expanding rapidly.
  • In particular
    The conference discussed many issues, in particular, climate change.

练习：填空

阅读下面的雅思写作Task 1范文，并选择合适的语篇标记词填入空白处。每个空白可能有多个正确答案。

The chart illustrates changes in energy production in a country between 1995 and 2005. 1, it is clear that the overall energy production increased significantly during this period.

2, coal was the main energy source in 1995, accounting for 50% of total production. Natural gas and hydroelectric power each represented 20%, 3 nuclear power contributed the remaining 10%.

Over the following decade, significant changes occurred. Coal production increased to 60% by 2005, 4 maintaining its dominant position. 5, natural gas saw a considerable decline, dropping to just 10%. 6, hydroelectric power remained stable at 20%, 7 nuclear power doubled its share to 20%.

8, the most notable change was the growth in coal and nuclear power, 9 the reduction in natural gas. 10, the country became more reliant on coal as its primary energy source.

参考答案

以下是每个空白处可以填写的可能答案：

  • 1 Overall, Generally, Initially
  • 2 Firstly, To begin with, Initially
  • 3 while, whereas
  • 4 thus, thereby
  • 5 In contrast, However, On the other hand
  • 6 Meanwhile, In comparison
  • 7 while, whereas
  • 8 Overall, In summary, To sum up
  • 9 and, along with
  • 10 As a result, Consequently, Therefore
