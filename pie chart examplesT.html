雅思饼图范例分析 (教师版)

以下范例包含详细的教师点评，旨在帮助学生理解数据描述的优劣。

优秀范例 (Good Examples)

旅游目的 (Purpose of Travel)

描述：

The main reason for travel was to go on holiday, accounting for almost half of the total trips at 48%. This was followed by visiting family and friends, which made up a quarter of the total. In contrast, travel for business and education purposes constituted just 15% and 12% respectively, highlighting their minor role.

2005年互联网使用 (Internet Use in 2005)

描述：

In 2005, 'Social Media' was the most popular internet activity, accounting for 40% of the total. Following this was 'Online Gaming', which represented 30% of internet usage. The remaining 15% was for 'Online Shopping', making it the least popular activity among the three.

2015年互联网使用 (Internet Use in 2015)

描述：

By 2015, the percentage of people using 'Social Media' for internet activities had risen dramatically to 65%. In stark contrast, 'Online Gaming' saw a sharp decline, falling to just 10% of total use. 'Online Shopping' also experienced significant growth, jumping from 15% to 25% over the decade.

不良范例 (Bad Examples)

家庭开支 (Household Expenses)

描述：

The chart shows some data. The biggest part is food, which is 35%. Rent is 25%. Utilities is 15%. Transport is 15%. Other is 10%. Food is the biggest category. Other is the smallest.

教师点评：

  • 过于口语化： 语言过于简单，如 "The chart shows some data." 和 "The biggest part is food." 不符合学术写作要求。
  • 数据罗列： 仅仅列出了所有数据点，缺乏分析和归纳，没有将数据与观点结合。
  • 重复信息： 多次提及 "biggest" 和 "smallest"，表达方式单一且重复。

年轻人购物方式 (Young Adults' Shopping)

描述：

Young people do a lot of online shopping. The percentage is 60%. It is very interesting.

教师点评：

  • 主观判断： 句子结尾使用 "It is very interesting." 这种主观评价，在雅思写作中是不恰当的。
  • 语言不精确： 使用 "a lot of" 和 "young people" 等口语化表达，应使用更正式的词汇，如 "the majority of..." 或 "the younger age group"。

老年人购物方式 (Older Adults' Shopping)

描述：

Old people also do online shopping, 30%. Old people like to go to stores, 70%. So old people shop more in stores. It is a good thing to shop in stores.

教师点评：

  • 语言不精确： 使用 "Old people" 和 "shop more in stores" 这种口语化表达，应使用更正式的词汇，如 "the older age group" 或 "prefer to shop in stores"。
  • 缺乏逻辑衔接： 句子之间缺乏有效的连接词，如 "in contrast" 或 "whereas"，使得描述显得生硬且零散。
  • 主观判断： 句子结尾使用 "It is a good thing to shop in stores." 这种主观评价，在雅思写作中是不恰当的。
